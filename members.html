Annie "Gondi" Rendall '13

President

Annie Rendall hails from South Burlington, Vermont where she grew up skiing in the Green Mountains, swimming in Lake Champlain, and singing with her high school jazz band on Church Street. If you are currently looking for Annie your best bet is to check Rocks rehearsal at the Hop, the Dartmouth Skiway, or the Moore Psychology Building. In addition to the Rocks, Annie is a member of the Women's Alpine Ski Team, and has a major in psychology. Through the amazingly empowered group of women that make up the Rockapellas, Annie has met some of her best friends and has realized her passion for social justice. She is unsure of what she wants to do after college, but knows that music will always be a huge part of her life!

Jamie "Mater" Mercado '15


Jamie Mercado is a food enthusiast (despite her many allergies) from Fairfield, Connecticut. She cannot whistle or do a satisfactory cartwheel. As a child, she was deprived and did not watch any television or movies that were not sports-related, and as a result, she receives many looks of horror each day when people find out that she has not yet seen The Lion King in its entirety. She has played ninja at the Louvre and under the Eiffel Tower. She knows all the words to the Kim Possible theme song, but only in French, and will serenade you, always with a poor accent, upon request. While she waits to figure out where her life is headed, she masquerades as a Dartmouth student, studying tantalizing stuff and transcendental things. Unless you have a plethora of time, do not ask her to name her favorite band, because she will begin to list at least twenty groups you've never heard of, and they'll be different every time you query. She has been known to sporadically burst into song for no reason at all, so don't be surprised if that's a thing that happens.
